The Research Division was established in 1943 and is located at Centeno, East Trinidad. Our mission is to Conduct Agricultural Research and related activities consistent with National Agricultural plans and policies.

The division manages 793 hectarces of land. The staff profile includes:

  • Agronomists, Entomologists, Plant Pathologists, Soil Scientists, Livestock Officers, Biochemist, Analytical Chemist.
  • Agric-Officers, Agric-Assistants, Sci Assts, Draughtsmen, Plant Quarantine Officers.
  • Research and Service Laboratories


    • Research programmes:

    Agronomic and Post Harvest Studies on Vegetable Crops, Root Crops, Tropical Fruits, Cocoa cropName Coffee, Rice, Ornamentals and Medicinal Plants

    Varietal Trials, Crop Ecology, Soil Management, Fertilizer Trials, Crop Protection, Post Harvest, Biochemistry.
